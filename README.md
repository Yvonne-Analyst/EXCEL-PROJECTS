## Overall Performance

Total Sales: $0.36M generated across all outlets.

Average Sales per Item: $143, indicating moderate ticket size.

Total Items Sold: 2,502 items.

Average Rating: 4.0 ‚≠ê, suggesting good customer satisfaction but room for improvement.

üè™ Outlet Performance Insights
Outlet Size

High-sized outlets dominate sales, contributing ~59% ($210.4K) of total revenue.

Medium outlets contribute ~20% ($70.8K).

Small outlets generate the least at ~21% ($76.0K).
Insight: Scaling or optimizing high-sized outlets yields the highest ROI.

Outlet Location

Tier 2 locations outperform others, generating $356.7K, almost the entire revenue.

Tier 1 and Tier 3 locations contribute minimally.
Insight: Blinkit performs strongest in semi-urban or emerging markets (Tier 2).

Outlet Establishment Trend

Sales peaked around 2017 (~$121K).

A decline is observed after 2017, reaching ~$117K by 2020.
Insight: Market saturation, competition, or operational challenges may have impacted post-2017 growth.

## Key Business Recommendations

Double down on Tier 2 locations and large outlets.

Expand inventory and marketing for snack foods and fresh produce.

Investigate post-2017 sales decline for operational or market causes.

Introduce targeted health campaigns if low-fat product adoption is a goal.

Strengthen customer experience to push ratings beyond 4.0.
